# Getting Started with Notion

Notion is a free web and mobile app for taking notes, managing tasks, 
and organising information — all in one place. This guide will help 
you set up your first page in under 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Create a New Page

In the top left corner of the sidebar, click the **+ New Page** button.  
[Screenshot: sidebar with + New Page button highlighted]

## Step 2: Add a Title

Type your page title in the large heading field at the top of the page.  
[Screenshot: empty page with cursor in heading field]

## Step 3: Add Text and a Bullet List

Click below your heading and start typing to add text.  
To add a bullet list, type `/` and select **Bulleted List** from the menu.  
[Screenshot: slash command menu open]

## Step 4: Share Your Page

In the top right corner, click **Share**.  
- To invite someone directly, enter their email address and click **Invite**.  
- To share a link, click **Copy Link** and send it to anyone.  
[Screenshot: Share menu open]
